The Sacred Bible

Este aplicativo es el clásico juego del ahorcado con un enfoque diferente. Tienes diferente categorías para probar tus conocimientos. Rescue the accused of his unfortunate fate with your knowledge. Do not let the ninja be near the gallows and perform the execution. You can choose different topics: Geography, Painters, Writers, Animals, Sports, Flowers, Trees, Minerals, Chemistry, Actors. Three levels of difficulty. You can play with 20, 30 or 40 words. At the moment only in spanish.
